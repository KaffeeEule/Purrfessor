# Purrfessor
Purrfessor is a tiny, playful web-based program that brings advice, humor, and motivation straight to your screen. It’s designed as a single-page interactive experience, perfect for quick inspiration, a laugh, or a gentle nudge when you’re stuck.

Features

Click "Make Me Laugh" - Random Humor & Jokes – Cat-themed puns, clever quips, or silly one-liners whenever you need a smile.

Praise & Encouragement – Click “Praise me” to receive cheerful feedback, animations, and fun cat reactions.

Click "Purrfessor" – Quick, inspiring quotes for work, focus, or life.

Click "Surprise Me" – Press the button for a random tip, joke, or motivational boost.

Interface in Antigravity

Animated black Cat Avatar – Walks around the screen, sleeps, eats, plays. Reacts to your actions: nods, winks, swishes its tail, or purrs.

Simple Dialogue Box – Type questions or click buttons to get advice, jokes, or praise.

Interactive Buttons – Options like “Purrfessor”, “Make Me Laugh,” “Praise Me,” or “Random Surprise.”

Fun Animations & Sounds – Lightweight visuals, cartoon style and subtle meows enhance engagement.

Why Purrfessor?
It’s a tiny, interactive web-program that combines fun, guidance, and encouragement. Perfect for anyone who wants a mix of humor, wisdom, and cat-powered motivation right on their screen.
